Introduction

Animal Crossing is a series i've enjoyed for many years, despite only having the ability to play the games in recent times, so I said why not and made a webshrine about it!! It's just such a comfy game and it really helps me with stuff irl, like stress.

Since discovering the series, i have watched video essay after video essay in a bingefest, absorbing knowledge bits of every single game! I've decided then to compile it on a webshrine!

I hope this shrine can bring across the feelings i have toward this wonderful series!
